Who we are

Rebuilding how India's workforce finds work

Vahan AI is a YC-backed startup that has built India's most powerful AI-driven hiring platform for blue-collar and grey-collar workers. We use intelligent automation, conversational AI, and deep hiring data to connect millions of workers with employers at a scale and speed that was never possible before.


Our platform automates the full hiring cycle: sourcing, screening, onboarding, and payroll management. What used to take weeks now happens in 2 to 3 days. We work with India's largest consumer platforms and are the biggest manpower partner by volume for most of our clients.

Workforce recruitment

About the founder

Meet Madhav Krishna

MK
Madhav Krishna
Founder and CEO, Vahan AI

Madhav is a computer engineer with an AI degree from Columbia University. Before founding Vahan, he spent seven years working at startups in the US, including Jetsetter. He returned to India with a clear sense of purpose: use technology to create real economic opportunity for people who have been left behind by the digital economy.

The founding moment came in the winter of 2014. A chance encounter outside his Delhi home with a child in poverty stopped him in his tracks. That moment led to three years of searching for the right product before landing on what Vahan is today. He started with a voice-based English learning platform, pivoted to WhatsApp-based workforce training, and finally found his breakthrough in 2018: a recruitment marketplace that digitized local placement agents instead of replacing them.

"Something just snapped inside me. I was literally in tears. Because I realized how fortunate I was to be handing the food out and not receiving it."
